# HOnly — Reference Apps

*June 2026 · competitive & design reference*

Apps HOnly learns from — and what it deliberately avoids. HOnly is adjacent to friendship/dating apps but a different category: **conversation-first, staying online is valid.**

---

## Bumble BFF / Bumble For Friends

Friendship app · [reference video](https://youtu.be/IuzaZsK3Y-o)

**Study:**
| Area | What |
|---|---|
| Onboarding | How it asks what kind of people you want to meet |
| Profile | Presenting yourself for friendship, not romance |
| Discovery | Browsing by shared interests / nearby |
| Safety | Reporting, blocking, verification/trust signals |
| Positioning | "Find your people" — friendship-first |

**Borrow:** friendship-first onboarding · warm, safe tone · interest-based profile fields · discovery by shared interests · reporting/blocking patterns.

**Don't copy:** swiping as the core experience · pushing offline meetings · location/distance as the main value · "meet IRL" framing.

**HOnly difference:** Bumble BFF makes friends, often in real life. HOnly connects through conversation first — staying online is completely valid.
